Bio

José A. López is a senior at Tufts University, originally from Pasadena, California. This upcoming spring he will receive his B.S. in Engineering Psychology/Human Factors. During his time at Tufts University, José has worked a wide variety of jobs, spanning from IT customer service to UX Designer, giving him a plethora of experience in dealing with human factors in a range of different products and technologies. His work has positively impacted people within Tufts and outside of the university, and he takes pride in having the opportunity to help others. When José is not in class or partaking in UX work, he enjoys volunteering at animal shelters, practicing martial arts, or playing guitar in his band.
