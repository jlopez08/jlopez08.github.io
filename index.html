About Me



I am a senior at Tufts University, originally from Pasadena, California. This upcoming summer I will receive my B.S. in Engineering Psychology/Human Factors. Peoples' interactions with technology in their everyday and professional lives fascinate me, and I enjoy thinking about the UI/UX design aspects that go into creating those technologies. I am interested in taking problems people encounter and designing user-centered solutions that provide an answer to those problems. I take pride in having the opportunity to help others, and have found that the field of UI/UX design gives me an opportunity to do just that.

I am focused on obtaining an entry-level or internship position as a UI/UX designer at an organization where I can continue to design user experiences that not only look visually appealing, but also serve a practical purpose for the end user.
