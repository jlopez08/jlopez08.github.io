Social Good Marketing, Inc.


Social Good Marketing Inc. is an organization that provides integrated marketing services to small and medium businesses and nonprofits. In this project, I designed a website to help expand their web presence, and for potential clients, partners, and interns looking to find information about the company.


Service Rendered: Website Design


Women in Computer Science


Women in Computer Science is a group at Tufts University dedicated to empowering female-identifying individuals in computer science through community and professional development. For this project, I put together a high fidelity mockup of a potential website for the group. This project was part of a design competition series at the Fall 2016 Tufts Polyhack Hackathon, and was completed in 3 hours as per the time alloted for this segment of the competition. I was awarded Top Web Designer at the hackathon for this design.


Service Rendered: Website Design



ULink


ULink is a startup dedicated to helping students in community colleges achieve their dreams of attended four-year colleges and universities by helping guide them through the transferring process. They seek to do this through a mobile app and college adminstration website that helps link students to mentors and college adminstrators. In this project, I designed the beta app and website that was tested with students, mentors and administrator test users.


Service Rendered: Beta Mobile App & Website Design
